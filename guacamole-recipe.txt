Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients:
2 large ripe avocados
2 roma tomatoes (optional)
sea salt
1 tsp Lemon Juice
2 tsp Lime Juice


1. Cut avocados in half length-wise and remove seeds.
2. 'Cube' the meat of the avocados and scoop into a bowl.
3. Chop tomatoes into 1/4" cubes if you like them in your guacamole
4. Combine all ingredients, mix until it reaches desired consistency.
